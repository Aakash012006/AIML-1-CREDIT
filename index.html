Startup Vision

What I'm Building Toward

AI-Native Supply Chain OS

Global supply chain is a $19 trillion industry running on spreadsheets, gut feelings, and outdated ERP systems. When a ship gets stuck in the Suez Canal, companies find out on the news — not from their own systems.

I'm building toward a platform that treats supply chain as a living graph: where every node (supplier, warehouse, route) has an AI agent watching it, every edge has a risk score, and the whole thing surfaces decisions — not dashboards — to the humans who need to act.

The thesis: predictive over reactive. Small and mid-market companies should have the same supply chain intelligence that Amazon has. Starting with demand forecasting + anomaly detection — where the bleeding is most visible.
